Welcome to Young Stars of Dugrem.

It is said that history has no boundaries and tradition is not created by a single person, that’s why 45 years of this traditional event has been maintained till date by the residents of the smallest ward in the village of Agassaim called Dugrem. This is the level of enthusiasm shown by the youth of Dugrem to entertain the whole of Goa on the 10th of August every year on the feast of our patron St.Lawrence. This event was started in early 1961 which was not counted. It’s an event that ancestral bhatkars organized to show off the likesuperiority of their coconuts in their plots.
